The Testing Begins

To determine how to get the most performance out of DominionOS, we will be testing different CyanogenMod-based builds with UberTC, DragonTC, and a combination of custom kernels for both osprey and titan (and maybe a few more if we can get testers). The winning combination will be determined by the amount of useful features in the kernel, and Antutu Benchmark scores.
